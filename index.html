How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich:

  1. Grab two slices of bread.
  2. Grab peanut butter, jelly, and knife.
  3. Spread peanut butter on one slide of bread with knife.
  4. Spread jelly on other slice of bread with knife.
  5. Place jelly-coated bread slice on top of peanut butter-coated bread slice.
  6. Enjoy!
